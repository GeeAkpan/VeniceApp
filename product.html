Our Product


Welcome to our company, where we are dedicated to bringing clean and sustainable cooking solutions to emerging markets in Sub-Saharan Africa. Our solar induction cookers utilize cutting-edge technology to provide an efficient and eco-friendly alternative to traditional charcoal and wood-burning stoves. We believe in making clean cooking accessible to everyone, which is why we offer a pay-as-you-go model that allows our customers to pay for their cookers in affordable installments. This ensures that even those with limited resources can benefit from the advantages of clean cooking and reduce their carbon footprint. At our core, we are committed to creating a better future for Sub-Saharan Africa by improving health, reducing deforestation, and promoting economic growth through sustainable solutions. Join us on this journey and be a part of the movement towards a brighter, cleaner, and more equitable future

Vencooker Single Burner

Compact yet powerful, the Vencooker Single Burner is the perfect single-burner induction cooktop for small kitchens or personal use. Its stainless steel build ensures durability, while its efficient heating technology delivers exceptional cooking performance. Enjoy the freedom of solar power with this versatile and eco-friendly appliance.

Vencooker Two Burner

This powerful induction cooktop features dual cooking zones, including a booster function for rapid heating. Its sleek ceramic glass surface is easy to clean and maintain, while smart feature like auto-pan detection that offer unparalleled convenience. Powered by the sun, the Vencooker Two Burner is not just a kitchen appliance but a sustainable lifestyle choice.
